Create A Spark

An international initiative by Unge Vil — the Norwegian youth‑led non‑profit dedicated to building Network, Passion and Experience for people aged 13–26 years.

What is Create A Spark?

A series of fast‑paced “marathons” that push you to start and finish bold ideas on a tight deadline.

Current concepts include:

Movie Marathon

A Film Marathon is a low‑threshold activity for youth and young adults. In a Film Marathon, we use what we have available — for example a mobile phone, a webcam, or any other equipment — since the focus is on mastering interpretation and storytelling, rather than the technical quality of the video itself. During the workshop, participants receive training in everything from script writing to acting and camera work, and they are supported by mentors throughout the entire marathon.

The main task is to interpret a word or theme that is handed out on the first day, and over the course of the week the participants collaborate to create a short film based on their interpretation. We conclude the marathon with a collective screening, celebrating the achievement as a small film festival, where the participants also receive constructive feedback from mentors. The purpose is to create a sense of accomplishment and to strengthen collaboration.

Music Marathon

Example sprint: write, record and release an original track in just 7 days, supported by professional producers and mentors.

Across all tracks you’ll team up with peers, get real‑time guidance from industry mentors and premiere your finished work to a global audience.
