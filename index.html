Create A Spark

We run high‑energy marathons where young people make real things: songs, short films, comics, games, community projects—fast. Modern tools, great mentors, and serious fun.

See upcoming marathons

What it is

Intense, weekend‑long sprints focused on making. Learn, build, and showcase—together.

Who it's for

Youth & young adults (13–26) and mentors who love music, film, tech, art, and storytelling.

Why it works

Clear goals, supportive mentors, and a stage at the end. You leave with something you made.

Marathons

Pick a format. Bring your spark. We supply mentors, gear and vibes.
