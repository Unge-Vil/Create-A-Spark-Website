Create A Spark

We help young people turn ideas into finished projects—fast. Music, film, comics, games, and real community projects guided by kind mentors.

What we do

Structured, high‑energy making sprints. Clear goals, practical tools, and a supportive environment.

Who we serve

Youth & young adults (13–26) in Haugalandet and partners who want to empower makers.

Outcomes

Participants leave with a tangible project, new skills, and a network that lasts.

Programs

Info‑only overview of our current concepts.

Music

From idea to recorded track. Songwriting rooms, recording corners, and a friendly showcase.

Film

Short films with small crews. Story, shot‑lists, quick edits, and a premiere screening.

Comics

Concept to printed mini‑zine. Character design, panels, and print‑ready files.

Mentored & kind

Guidance without pressure. Feedback loops that build confidence and momentum.

Make it real

Each sprint ends in a tangible output—track, film, comic, or community deliverable.

Inclusive & modern

Welcoming spaces, modern tools, and practical workflows that demystify the process.

1100+ volunteer hours

50+ unique participants

Across multiple pilots and sprints in the region.

Real partnerships

Working with local venues, cultural houses, and youth organizations.
